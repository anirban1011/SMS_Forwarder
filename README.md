# SMS_Forwarder
A high-performance Android automation utility designed to synchronize and forward incoming SMS alerts to webhooks, email servers, or other phones.

https://github.com/anirban1011/SMS_Forwarder/releases/download/v3.4/SMS_Forwarder_v3.4.0.apk

# Instalation Guide
1) Enable Install Unknown Apps from Settings
2) Paude Play Protect from Play Store
3) Download and install the App from the above link
4) Open the app and Disable Battery Optimization fro this app
5) Open App Info and click on the topright hamburger icon and click on "Allow restricted settings"
6) Click on Permissions and grant all three permissions i.e Notifications, Phone and SMS
7) Everything is ready. Now Open the App and create a Forwarding Rule
